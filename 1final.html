Game Story

The story unfolds on Sunny HAVEN, an isolated island where clones make up the primary workforce. Under a seemingly ideal system, there is no oppression or overtime, and companies provide abundant, delicious food—at least on the surface. Each clone works only eight hours a day, and after accumulating enough time, they are promised entry into Sunny HAVEN’s core: a so-called paradise where work is no longer needed, and happiness awaits. This dream of freedom drives every clone forward.

In the first playthrough, players easily complete the main storyline and step into “paradise.” In the second playthrough, although the events appear largely the same, subtle cracks begin to show, leading players to make a different choice upon reaching paradise. By the third playthrough, the truth becomes unavoidable. Players seek out the inconsistencies, awaken others to reality, and ultimately forge their path toward one of three possible endings: revolution, integration, or escape.
